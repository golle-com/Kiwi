## [Just The Recipe](https://justtherecipe.app)

Paste any recipe URL. It strips out the life story, the ads, and the popups. You get only the ingredients and the instructions. Built by one developer who was tired of scrolling.

## [Internet Archive](https://archive.org)

Every website that ever existed, archived. Read articles that got deleted. See what a website looked like in 1999. The internet's permanent memory. Free forever.

## [Photopea](https://photopea.com)

Full Photoshop in your browser. Opens PSD files, layers, vector graphics. One developer built it in his spare time. Adobe charges $55 a month for the same thing.

## [Have I Been Pwned](https://haveibeenpwned.com)

Type your email. It tells you every data breach your account has been in. Built by a security researcher in a weekend. Now the official tool used by governments and Firefox.

## [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app)

Google built this to compress images in your browser. Drag your photo in, watch it shrink from 4MB to 200KB in real time. No data leaves your device. Google built it and gave it away for free.

## [AlternativeTo](https://alternativeto.net)

Type any paid app. It shows you every free alternative, ranked by the people who recommended it. Find a free version of anything. Built as a crowdsourced directory.

## [Remove.bg](https://remove.bg)

Upload any photo. The background disappears in 5 seconds. What used to take 20 minutes in Photoshop now takes one click. Free for most uses.

## [TinyWow](https://tinywow.com)

200+ free browser tools in one place. Convert, compress, edit, merge and split PDFs, images and videos. The Swiss Army knife of the internet. Everything works in your browser.

## [Excalidraw](https://excalidraw.com)

Draw diagrams, flowcharts and wireframes in your browser in a hand-drawn style. Real-time collaboration built in. Built by an open-source community. Free forever. Figma charges $15 a month per seat for the same thing.

## [Sleepyti.me](https://sleepyti.me)

Enter what time you need to wake up. It calculates exactly when you should fall asleep based on 90-minute sleep cycles. So you wake up between cycles, not in the middle of one. Built by one person in a weekend.

## [ManualsLib](https://manualslib.com)

The manual for every product ever made, in one place. TV, washing machine, camera, coffee maker. Free PDF. Works for products from 30 years ago. Saves you from throwing something away just because you lost the instructions.

## [JustDeleteMe](https://justdeleteme.xyz)

A directory of every major website with a direct link to their account deletion page. Rated by how hard they make it to leave. Built because companies hide this on purpose.

## [RTK](https://github.com/rtk-ai/rtk)

CLI proxy that reduces LLM token consumption by 60-90% on common dev commands. Single Rust binary, zero dependencies. Works with Claude, Copilot, VSCode, Cursor, Gemini, Codex and many more.
