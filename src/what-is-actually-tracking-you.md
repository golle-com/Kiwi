## IP address
- Every website sees your real IP address.
- Reveals your approximate location and ISP.
- VPN hides this, but not everything else.
- WebRTC can leak real IP even with VPN active.

## Browser fingerprinting
Your browser tells websites:
- Operating system and version
- Browser type and version
- Screen resolution and color depth
- Installed fonts and plugins
- Timezone and language settings
- Hardware details (GPU, CPU cores)

This combination is unique to you.

- 87% of browsers have a unique fingerprint.
- VPN does not protect against this.
- Incognito mode does not protect against this.

## Cookies and trackers
- Third-party cookies follow you across websites.
- Facebook Pixel tracks you even without Facebook open.
- Google Analytics is on 85%+ of all websites.
- Logging into any account reveals identity.
- Even private browsing saves cookies during session.

## Device identifiers
- MAC address identifies your device on local networks.
- IMEI number identifies your phone globally.
- Advertising ID (GAID/IDFA) tracks you across apps.
- Canvas fingerprint: how your GPU renders graphics.
- Audio fingerprint: how your device processes sound.

Your IP is just one of 20+ ways you are being tracked right now.
