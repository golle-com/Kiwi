1. **[Instaloader](https://github.com/instaloader/instaloader)** - Pull full metadata and posts from any public Instagram account.
1. **[Toutatis](https://github.com/megadose/toutatis)** - Extract phone numbers and emails from Instagram account data.
1. **[Snscrape](https://github.com/JustAnotherArchivist/snscrape)** - Scrape public social media content without API keys.
1. **[Arctic Shift]()** - Access and search historical Reddit datasets.
1. **[Mosint](https://github.com/alpkeskin/mosint)** - Run automated OSINT checks on email addresses from the terminal.
1. **[GAU](https://github.com/lc/gau)** - Fetch known URLs for a domain from multiple archives.
1. **[Waybackurls](https://github.com/tomnomnom/waybackurls)** - Pull URLs recorded by the Wayback Machine for a domain.
1. **[Photon](https://github.com/s0md3v/Photon)** - Crawl websites and extract links and metadata quickly.
1. **[Crosslinked](https://github.com/m8sec/CrossLinked)** - Extract employee names from LinkedIn-related sources.
1. **[URLScan](https://urlscan.io/)** - Scan a URL and inspect all requests it makes.
1. **[Datashare](https://datashare.icij.org/)** - Search leaked documents and large document collections.
1. **[Zeek](https://zeek.org/)** - Turn network traffic into structured logs for analysis.
1. **[Gitleaks](https://github.com/gitleaks/gitleaks)** - Scan Git repositories for exposed secrets and API keys.
1. **[OpenRefine](https://openrefine.org/)** - Reconcile and clean messy investigation datasets.
1. **[Cytoscape](https://cytoscape.org/)** - Map relationship networks as interactive graphs.
1. **[Certspotter](https://sslmate.com/certspotter/)** - Monitor domains for newly issued SSL certificates.
1. **[Subfinder](https://github.com/projectdiscovery/subfinder)** - Discover subdomains from passive sources.
1. **[pl]()** - Reveal hidden GPS coordinates and camera metadata in images.
1. **[Profil3r](https://github.com/Rog3rSm1th/Profil3r)** - Find accounts tied to a username across platforms.
1. **[Ignorant](https://github.com/megadose/ignorant)** - Check which services a phone number may be registered on.
1. **[SDR++](https://www.sdrpp.org/)** - Decode live radio signals from multiple sources.
1. **[ODCrawler]()** - Search publicly exposed files in open directories.
1. **[Tgstat](https://tgstat.com/)** - Search and analyze public Telegram channels and posts.
1. **[Discord History Tracker]()** - Archive and search Discord server chat history.
1. **[Osintgram](https://github.com/Datalux/Osintgram)** - Run interactive analysis on public Instagram accounts.
1. **[Maileat]()** - Find email addresses linked to usernames online.
1. **[Zen]()** - Find possible email intelligence tied to GitHub accounts.
1. **[Pastebin Search](https://psbdmp.ws/)** - Search public paste-style leaks and exposed data.
1. **[Orbit]()** - Trace transactions linked to cryptocurrency activity.
1. **[Little Namap]()** - Map flight routes and track aircraft data.
1. **[QGIS](https://qgis.org/)** - Overlay investigation data on real-world maps.
1. **[IVRE](https://ivre.rocks/)** - Build a private reconnaissance and device indexing database.
1. **[Webtech](https://github.com/ShielderSec/webtech)** - Fingerprint website technology stacks.
1. **[Changedetection](https://changedetection.io/)** - Get alerts when target webpages update.
1. **[Datasette](https://datasette.io/)** - Turn CSVs or databases into searchable explorers.
1. **[Gephi](https://gephi.org/)** - Visualize complex entity relationships as interactive networks.
1. **[Volatility](https://www.volatilityfoundation.org/)** - Extract forensic artifacts from memory dumps.
1. **[CyberChef](https://gchq.github.io/CyberChef/)** - Decode, decrypt, and analyze suspicious data.
